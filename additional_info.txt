This data package includes data from MIT-WHOI Joint Program (JP) cruises funded by WHOI Academic Programs Office, with Chief Scientists Glen Gawarkiewicz (AR22 and AR38) and Joel Llopiz (AR32). We'd like to thank the Ocean Observatories Initiative (OOI) for collaboration on other cruises on R/V Neil Armstrong included in this package.

Special thanks to Paul Henderson at the WHOI Nutrient Analytical Facility for analyzing the samples collected for this data package.
